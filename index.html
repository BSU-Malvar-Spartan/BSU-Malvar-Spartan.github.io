Who We Are

About the Spartans

BSU Malvar Spartan is the official GitHub organization of the technology-focused student community at Batangas State University — Malvar Campus. We bring together passionate students to build real-world projects, sharpen their skills, and contribute to open source.

Our members collaborate across disciplines — software engineering, data science, UI/UX design, and more — to create impactful solutions for the university and the broader community.
